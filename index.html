Petr Spelda

Assistant Professor at the Department of Security Studies, Charles University, Prague.

I work in AI safety and epistemology. Most of my works deal with inductive inference in various learning setups. I believe that formal study of inductive inference is important for AI safety.

I collaborate with Vit Stritecky, my colleague from Charles, and John Symons from The University of Kansas. I am also a fellow at the Center for Cyber-Social Dynamics at KU.
